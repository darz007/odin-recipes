Schnitzel

Description

A schnitzel is a thin slice of meat. The meat is usually thinned by pounding with a meat tenderizer. Most commonly, the meat is breaded before frying.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breast
  2. Dip the chicken in flour
  3. Dip the chicken in beated eggs
  4. Dip the chicken in the breadcrumbs mixed in with salt
  5. Lightly fry in a pan with oil
